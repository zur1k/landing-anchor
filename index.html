ABOUT THe project

Stunning newly done Contemporary Cape Cod Estate in coveted Bird Streets.

Home to LA’s top celebs and above famed Sunset Strip. Catering to quintessential in/outdoor SoCal living, this amazing street-to-street 7,500 SQFT compound sits on a huge 13k SQFT lot. Designer-done 5 bedrooms/8 bathrooms masterpiece comes equipped with a state-of-the-art movie theater, wine cellar and incredible guest house with sauna.

Completely gated pool area surrounded by lush yard, which is rare to find in the Bird Streets. 3-car garage and plenty of off-street parking make this an entertainer’s dream home. Soaring ceilings, magnificent chandeliers, and beautiful staircase create a grand entrance to impress. Floor plan flows openly between gourmet kitchen, fireplace, and patio with disappearing Fleetwood Pocket doors.
